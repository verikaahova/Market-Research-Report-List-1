<p><p>The nuclear primary pipe market encompasses various reactor types, each with distinct characteristics. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) use high-pressure water to transfer heat, while Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) generate steam directly in the reactor core. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) utilize heavy water as a moderator, while Gas-Cooled Reactors (GCR) use gas as a coolant. Liquid Metal Fast Breeder Reactors (FBR) employ liquid metal for cooling, and Light Water Graphite Reactors (LWGR) combine light water and graphite for neutron moderation. Each type caters to specific operational needs and market demands.</p></p>
