<p><ul><li>Batch Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF dryers, or radio frequency dryers, are categorized mainly into batch and continuous types. Batch-type RF dryers process a fixed amount of material at a time, making them ideal for small-scale operations that require flexibility and quality control. In contrast, continuous-type RF dryers facilitate a steady flow of materials through the drying chamber, enhancing efficiency and throughput for larger operations. Both types leverage radio frequency technology to ensure uniform heating and moisture removal, serving diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
