<p><p>The vacuum deposition service market encompasses techniques such as Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). PVD involves the physical transfer of material to form thin films, commonly used for coatings in electronics and optics due to its high purity and adhesion. CVD, on the other hand, uses chemical reactions to deposit materials, ideal for producing uniform coatings in various industries, including semiconductors and photovoltaics. Both methods are essential for enhancing material properties and functionalities.</p></p>
