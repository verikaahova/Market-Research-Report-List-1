<p><p>The asphalt adhesive market is segmented into three main types: solvent type, emulsion type, and others. Solvent-based asphalt adhesives contain organic solvents, which enhance adhesion but may pose environmental concerns. Emulsion-type asphalt adhesives consist of asphalt mixed with water and emulsifying agents, offering easier application and lower toxicity. The 'others' category includes innovative formulations and products that do not fit into the first two types, catering to specialized applications or meeting specific performance requirements in construction and industrial uses.</p></p>
