<p><strong>The Bacterial Endotoxin Testing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bacterial Endotoxin Testing Service market includes three primary test types. The Gel Clot Endotoxin Test utilizes a gel-forming reaction to indicate endotoxin presence, offering a qualitative assessment. The Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, on the other hand, employs colorimetric reactions for quantitative results, allowing for sensitive detection of endotoxins. Lastly, the Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test measures the turbidity change in a sample as endotoxins react with a lysate, providing rapid quantitative analysis. Each method caters to different regulatory and sensitivity requirements in various industries.</p></p>
