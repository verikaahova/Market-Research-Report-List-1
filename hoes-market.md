<p><p>Hoes are gardening tools used for soil cultivation, weed control, and planting. </p><p>**Draw hoes** feature a broad, flat blade and are used for turning over soil and creating furrows. **Scuffle hoes**, with their sharp, angled blades, excel at weeding by slicing beneath the soil surface. </p><p>Other types include **dutch hoes**, which have a similar action to scuffle hoes but are designed for precision weeding in tight spaces. Collectively, these hoes cater to various gardening needs, enhancing soil preparation and maintenance efficiency.</p></p>
