<p><ul><li>Vernier Depth Gauge</li><li>Digital Depth Gauge</li><li>Dial Depth Gauge</li><li>Defect Depth Gauge</li><li>Thread Depth Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The depth gauges market includes various types, each serving distinct measurement needs. Vernier depth gauges provide precision using a sliding scale and are popular in mechanical workshops. Digital depth gauges offer electronic readouts for ease of use and accuracy. Dial depth gauges utilize a dial indicator for straightforward depth readings. Defect depth gauges measure surface irregularities, vital in quality control, while thread depth gauges specifically assess thread depths in mechanical components, ensuring proper fit and function in engineering applications.</p></p>
