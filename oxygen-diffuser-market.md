<p><p>The oxygen diffuser market includes two main types: ceramic fine pore oxygen diffusers and cylinder ceramic oxygen diffusers. Ceramic fine pore oxygen diffusers feature small pores that facilitate efficient gas dispersion in aquatic environments, enhancing oxygen transfer in water treatment and aquaculture. In contrast, cylinder ceramic oxygen diffusers are designed with a cylindrical shape, offering a larger surface area for oxygen distribution, making them suitable for various applications, including industrial wastewater treatment and large-scale aquaculture systems.</p></p>
