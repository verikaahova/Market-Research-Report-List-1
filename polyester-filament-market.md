<p><p>The polyester filament market comprises various types, including primary silk, drawing the wire, and deformation of silk. Drawing the wire involves elongating these filaments to enhance their tensile properties and elasticity, making them suitable for various applications. Deformation of silk utilizes mechanical processes to alter filament characteristics, improving durability and performance in textiles and industrial uses, broadening the market's versatility.</p></p>
