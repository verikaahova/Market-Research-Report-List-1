<p><p>Heterojunction (HJT) solar panels are designed using a combination of thin-film and crystalline silicon technologies, enhancing efficiency and performance. The market is segmented into monofacial and bifacial cells. Monofacial HJT cells capture sunlight from one side, making them suitable for conventional installations. In contrast, bifacial HJT cells utilize both sides to absorb sunlight, increasing energy harvest, especially in reflective environments. The choice between these types depends on installation conditions, space availability, and desired energy output.</p></p>
