<p><p>The 3D glass testing market is categorized into two main types: Three Shaft and Two Shaft testing systems. Three Shaft testing involves a more complex setup with additional shafts for enhanced precision and is commonly used for high-end applications requiring detailed analysis of glass properties. In contrast, Two Shaft testing is simpler and often preferred for basic quality checks, offering efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the integrity and performance of 3D glass products in various industries.</p></p>
