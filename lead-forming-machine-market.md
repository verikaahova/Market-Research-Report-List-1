<p><strong>The Lead Forming Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Lead Forming Machine</li><li>Radial Lead Forming Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead forming machine market is divided primarily into two types: axial lead forming machines and radial lead forming machines. Axial lead forming machines are designed to bend and shape leads on electronic components that extend along the axis, allowing for efficient placement in circuit boards. In contrast, radial lead forming machines handle components with leads extending radially, optimizing space and connections on printed circuit boards. Both types enhance manufacturing efficiency and component integration in various electronic applications.</p></p>
