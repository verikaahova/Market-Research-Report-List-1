<p><p>The biomass fuel testing market encompasses various analytical types critical for assessing fuel quality. Calorific value measures the energy output during combustion, while ash content determines residual material post-combustion, affecting efficiency. Moisture content influences energy yield, as higher moisture reduces combustion efficiency. Sulphur content assessment is vital for minimizing emissions and environmental impact. Lastly, pH testing is crucial for understanding fuel acidity, which can affect combustion behavior and equipment corrosion. Each parameter ensures biomass fuels meet performance and regulatory standards.</p></p>
