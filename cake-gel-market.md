<p><p>The Cake Gel market can be categorized into two primary types: natural and synthetic. Natural cake gels are derived from plant-based sources, offering a clean label option for health-conscious consumers. They often contain ingredients like agar-agar or gelatin, enhancing moisture and texture in baked goods naturally. In contrast, synthetic cake gels are chemically engineered to provide specific functional properties, such as improved shelf life and consistency. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and applications within the food industry.</p></p>
