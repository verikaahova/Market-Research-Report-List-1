<p><p>Cotton spinning encompasses various market types, each employing unique techniques. Ring spun yarn is known for its strength and smoothness, making it popular in high-quality textiles. OE or rotor spun yarn offers greater production speed and is cost-effective, ideal for bulk applications. Core spun yarn combines strength and elasticity, suitable for specialty fabrics. Air jet spun yarn utilizes air for spinning, enhancing efficiency. Frictional spun yarn emphasizes low energy consumption and high production. Other technologies continue to evolve, expanding market options.</p></p>
