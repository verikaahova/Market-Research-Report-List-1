<p><p>Cardiac Rhythm Management Equipment encompasses devices that regulate heart rhythms, primarily including pacemakers and defibrillators. Pacemakers, implanted devices, deliver electrical impulses to maintain a normal heart rate in patients with bradycardia or irregular heartbeats. Defibrillators, on the other hand, are lifesaving devices designed to restore normal heart rhythm in cases of life-threatening arrhythmias through electric shocks. Both types play crucial roles in managing cardiac conditions, improving patient outcomes, and enhancing quality of life for individuals with heart disorders.</p></p>
