<p><ul><li>External Photoelectric Effect Detector</li><li>Internal Photoelectric Effect Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photoelectric Vacuum Detector market consists of two primary types: External Photoelectric Effect Detectors and Internal Photoelectric Effect Detectors. External detectors utilize light beams emitted outside the vacuum environment to detect changes in photonic activity, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, Internal Photoelectric Effect Detectors operate within the vacuum chamber itself, directly sensing photons generated by internal processes. Both types are crucial for high-precision measurement and monitoring in vacuum systems across industries like semiconductor manufacturing and research.</p></p>
