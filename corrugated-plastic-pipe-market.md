<p><p>The corrugated plastic pipe market features two primary types: single wall and double wall corrugated pipes. Single wall corrugated pipes are lightweight, flexible, and primarily used for drainage applications, offering cost-effective solutions in less demanding environments. In contrast, double wall corrugated pipes provide enhanced strength and durability, featuring an inner smooth layer for improved flow and an external corrugated layer for structural integrity. They are ideal for more challenging applications like stormwater management and sewage systems, offering superior performance and longevity.</p></p>
