<p><p>Epoxy adhesives are versatile bonding agents categorized into one-component, two-component, and other types. One-component epoxies are pre-mixed, ready-to-use adhesives that cure with heat or moisture, ideal for simplicity and efficiency. Two-component epoxies require mixing resin and hardener before application, offering strong, durable bonds for industrial and structural uses. Other types include UV-curable, flexible, or specialty epoxies tailored for unique applications. Each type serves specific needs, balancing ease of use, curing methods, and performance requirements across industries like construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
