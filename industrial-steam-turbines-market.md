<p><strong>The Industrial Steam Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reaction Turbines</li><li>Impulse Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial steam turbines market primarily consists of two types: reaction turbines and impulse turbines. Reaction turbines convert steam energy into mechanical energy through both pressure and velocity changes, utilizing the blade shape to create lift. In contrast, impulse turbines rely on high-velocity steam jets striking blades, generating torque from pressure drop across the turbine. Each type serves various industrial applications, with reaction turbines often favored for higher efficiency and impulse turbines suitable for simpler systems and specific operational conditions.</p></p>
