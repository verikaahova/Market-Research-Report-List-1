<p><strong>The Poultry Ventilation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural air Flow System</li><li>Mechanical Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The poultry ventilation systems market includes two primary types: natural airflow systems and mechanical ventilation systems. Natural airflow systems rely on passive methods such as openings, vents, and ducts to harness wind and temperature differences for air circulation, promoting a comfortable environment for birds. In contrast, mechanical ventilation systems utilize fans and blowers to control airflow and temperature actively, ensuring optimal conditions regardless of external weather. Both systems aim to enhance poultry health, productivity, and overall farm efficiency.</p></p>
