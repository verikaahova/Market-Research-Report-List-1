<p><p>The Non-Invasive Blood Pressure Monitors market consists of two main types: digital and mechanical. Digital monitors use advanced technology to display readings on an electronic screen, often featuring automatic inflation and deflation for ease of use. They are favored for their accuracy and user-friendly interfaces. In contrast, mechanical monitors employ a traditional method, using a sphygmomanometer and stethoscope, relying on manual inflation and auscultation. While mechanical devices require skill to operate, they remain trusted in clinical settings for their reliability.</p></p>
