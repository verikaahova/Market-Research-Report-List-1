<p><strong>The KF Titrator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The KF Titrator market is primarily categorized into two types: Coulometric Titration and Volumetric Titration. Coulometric titration involves measuring the amount of water by producing iodine through an electrochemical reaction, making it ideal for samples with low moisture content. In contrast, volumetric titration uses a reagent to quantify water content in samples with higher moisture levels, providing rapid and accurate results. Both methods are essential in industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals for moisture analysis.</p></p>
