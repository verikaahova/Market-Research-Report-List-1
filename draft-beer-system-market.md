<p><strong>The Draft Beer System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Draw</li><li>Long-Draw</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The draft beer system market is categorized into three main types: Direct-Draw, Long-Draw, and Air-Cooled systems. Direct-Draw systems feature short distances between the keg and tap, providing fresh beer but limited for larger establishments. Long-Draw systems use insulated lines for longer distances, allowing placement flexibility but requiring careful temperature management. Air-Cooled systems utilize chilled air to maintain beer temperature as it travels, suitable for various settings but less efficient compared to other methods. Each system caters to specific operational needs and configurations.</p></p>
