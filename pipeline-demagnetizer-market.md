<p><ul><li>Permanent-magnet Demagnetizer</li><li>Stored-energy Demagnetizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pipeline Demagnetizer market consists mainly of two types: Permanent-magnet Demagnetizers and Stored-energy Demagnetizers. Permanent-magnet Demagnetizers utilize constant magnetic fields to remove unwanted magnetism from pipelines, enhancing performance and reducing operational issues. In contrast, Stored-energy Demagnetizers leverage stored electrical energy to generate a strong, short-duration magnetic field, effectively neutralizing magnetism. Both types serve critical roles in industries where pipeline integrity and material properties are crucial, ensuring safety and efficiency in operations.</p></p>
