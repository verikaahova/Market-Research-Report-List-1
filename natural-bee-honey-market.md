<p><p>The natural bee honey market is primarily categorized into two types: polyfloral and monofloral honey. Polyfloral honey, also known as wildflower honey, is sourced from various floral sources, resulting in a diverse flavor profile and varying nutritional properties. In contrast, monofloral honey is derived from a single type of flower, offering distinct characteristics linked to that specific flora, often preferred for its unique taste and potential health benefits. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and culinary uses.</p></p>
