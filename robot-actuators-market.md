<p><ul><li>Linear Robot Actuators</li><li>Rotary Robot Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robot actuators market is primarily divided into two types: linear and rotary actuators. Linear robot actuators convert rotational motion into straight-line movement, commonly used in applications like robotic arms for pick-and-place operations. In contrast, rotary robot actuators provide rotational motion, enabling precise control in applications such as assembly and positioning tasks. Both types are essential for enhancing automation efficiency in various industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare, contributing to the growing demand for robotic solutions.</p></p>
