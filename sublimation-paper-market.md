<p><ul><li>Laser Printing</li><li>Inkjet Printing</li><li>Dye-sublimation Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sublimation paper market encompasses three primary printing types: laser printing, inkjet printing, and dye-sublimation printing. Laser printing uses heat to transfer toner onto the sublimation paper, suitable for full-color designs. Inkjet printing employs liquid inks that penetrate the paper, often used for smaller runs or detailed graphics. Dye-sublimation printing converts solid dye into gas, allowing for high-resolution images and vibrant colors on textiles and hard substrates. Each method serves distinct applications in the growing sublimation industry.</p></p>
