<p><ul><li>Halogen Flame</li><li>Halogen Free Flame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame retardant polypropylene granules are categorized into two primary types: halogen flame retardants and halogen-free flame retardants. Halogen flame retardants contain halogen elements, providing effective fire resistance but raising environmental and health concerns due to potential toxicity. Conversely, halogen-free flame retardants utilize alternative chemical compounds that offer fire protection without the associated risks of halogens, making them more environmentally friendly. Both types are crucial in various industries, including electronics, automotive, and construction, to enhance material safety standards.</p></p>
