<p><ul><li>Conventional Oilfield Degasser</li><li>Portable Oilfield Degasser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oilfield degasser market comprises primarily two types: conventional and portable degassers. Conventional oilfield degassers are fixed installations designed to handle large volumes of drilling fluids in a stationary setting, effectively removing gases like methane and hydrocarbons to enhance drilling efficiency. In contrast, portable oilfield degassers offer mobility, allowing operators to quickly address gas separation needs on-site. These units are particularly valuable in remote locations where flexibility and rapid deployment are essential for maintaining operational continuity and safety during drilling operations.</p></p>
