<p><p>The Premature Rupture of Membranes (PROM) testing market includes various types of tests to diagnose amniotic fluid leakage during pregnancy. The Nitrazine/pH test detects changes in vaginal pH, indicating fluid presence. The Ferning test identifies a ferning pattern under a microscope, confirming amniotic fluid. The Amnisure test utilizes a specific immunoassay to detect placental alpha-microglobulin-1 in vaginal secretions for accurate diagnosis. Other tests may include handheld devices or lab-based assays, enhancing the options available for clinicians.</p></p>
