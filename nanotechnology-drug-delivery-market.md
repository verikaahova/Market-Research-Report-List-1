<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Nanotechnology Drug Delivery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nanocrystals</li><li>Nanoparticles</li><li>Liposomes</li><li>Micelles</li><li>Nanotubes</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanotechnology drug delivery market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions. Nanocrystals enhance solubility and bioavailability of poorly water-soluble drugs. Nanoparticles, often polymer-based, enable targeted delivery and sustained release. Liposomes are lipid-based carriers that encapsulate drugs, improving stability and biocompatibility. Micelles are used to solubilize hydrophobic drugs in aqueous environments. Nanotubes, with their unique structure, facilitate drug transfer and cellular uptake. Other emerging technologies include dendrimers and airborne nanoparticles, broadening the scope of drug delivery solutions.</p></p>
