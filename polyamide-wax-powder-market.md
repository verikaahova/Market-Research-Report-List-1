<p><p>The polyamide wax powder market is categorized into two main types: water-soluble wax and oil-soluble wax. Water-soluble wax is designed to dissolve in water, making it suitable for applications in cosmetics, personal care, and adhesives, where ease of formulation is essential. In contrast, oil-soluble wax is compatible with oils and is often used in coatings, inks, and plastics for its excellent compatibility and performance in enhancing stability, gloss, and durability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
