<p><p>The 5G indoor small cells market encompasses two primary types: Sub-6GHz and mmWave. Sub-6GHz small cells utilize lower frequency bands, providing broader coverage and better penetration through walls, making them ideal for widespread indoor settings. In contrast, mmWave small cells operate at higher frequencies, offering faster data rates and increased capacity but with limited range and penetration. Together, these technologies enhance indoor connectivity, accommodating diverse user needs and supporting applications such as high-bandwidth streaming and smart-building solutions.</p></p>
