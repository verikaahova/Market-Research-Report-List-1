<p><p>The 3D dental scanner market includes two primary types: 3D dental light scanners and 3D dental laser scanners. 3D dental light scanners use structured light technology to capture comprehensive digital impressions of teeth and gums, offering a quick and accurate visualization of oral anatomy. In contrast, 3D dental laser scanners utilize laser technology to obtain detailed scans, providing high precision and reduced scanning time. Both types enhance dental workflows, improve treatment planning, and lead to better patient outcomes in dental practices.</p></p>
