<p><p>The high precision tools market comprises several key types, each serving distinct machining purposes. Lathe machines shape materials by rotating them against cutting tools, while milling machines remove material using rotating cutters on stationary workpieces. Drilling machines create holes with a rotating drill bit. Grinding machines refine surfaces through abrasion, ensuring tight tolerances. Boring machines enlarge existing holes with high precision, and gear cutters specifically produce gear profiles, enabling accurate gear manufacturing in various industrial applications.</p></p>
