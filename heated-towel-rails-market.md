<p><p>The heated towel rails market comprises two main types: electric and hydronic heated towel rails. Electric heated towel rails operate independently, using electrical heating elements to warm towels and are ideal for easy installation and flexibility in any bathroom. In contrast, hydronic heated towel rails connect to a home's existing plumbing system, utilizing hot water to provide heat, offering energy efficiency and consistent warmth. Both options enhance comfort and convenience by ensuring warm towels are always available.</p></p>
