<p><p>The polycrystalline diamond market primarily comprises two types: Polycrystalline Diamond Synthetics (PDSs) and Polycrystalline Diamond Compacts (PDCs). PDSs are engineered diamonds with a fine-grained structure, used in various industrial applications due to their enhanced hardness and thermal conductivity. PDCs, on the other hand, are diamond layers bonded to a substrate, widely employed in drilling operations and cutting tools for their durability and efficiency. Both types serve essential roles in industries like mining, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
