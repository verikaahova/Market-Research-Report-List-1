Solvent Type adhesives utilize organic solvents for dissolving polymer, offering strong adhesion and versatility. Emulsion Type adopts water-based formulations, providing environmental benefits and safety during application. Light Curing Type adhesives rely on ultraviolet or visible light to cure, enabling rapid setting and strong bonding. Each type caters to specific applications and industry needs, enhancing performance across various sectors.</p></p>
