<p><strong>The Auto Electromagnetic Compatibility Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Current Injection (BCI)</li><li>Conducted Emissions (CE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI)</li><li>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)</li><li>Radiated Emissions (RE)</li><li>Radiated Immunity (RI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Electromagnetic Compatibility Test market encompasses various testing types to evaluate vehicle electronics' resilience against electromagnetic interference. Bulk Current Injection (BCI) assesses susceptibility to conducted disturbances, while Conducted Emissions (CE) measures unwanted emissions from vehicles. Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE) and Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI) evaluate rapid electrical transients. Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) tests assess the vehicle's resilience to static electricity. Radiated Emissions (RE) and Radiated Immunity (RI) focus on emissions and immunity to external electromagnetic fields, ensuring regulatory compliance and reliable performance.</p></p>
