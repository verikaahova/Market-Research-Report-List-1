<p><p>The bristle brush market is divided into two main types: natural bristle and artificial bristle. Natural bristle brushes are made from animal hair, offering superior durability and effective bristle stiffness, making them ideal for delicate applications like painting or grooming. In contrast, artificial bristle brushes are crafted from synthetic materials, providing consistent quality and flexibility at a lower cost. These synthetic bristles are easier to clean and often exhibit enhanced resistance to moisture and wear, catering to various industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
