<p><p>The ePTFE vascular graft market is segmented into small caliber and large caliber categories, based on the diameter of the graft. Small caliber grafts, typically ranging from 4 to 6 mm, are used for procedures involving smaller vessels, such as coronary or peripheral artery bypasses. In contrast, large caliber grafts, usually exceeding 6 mm in diameter, are employed for larger vessel applications like aortic replacements or dialysis access. Both types are favored for their biocompatibility and durability in various surgical interventions.</p></p>
