<p><ul><li>Non-halogen Type</li><li>Halogen Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame-retarded ABS market is categorized into two main types: halogen type and non-halogen type. Halogen-type flame retardants incorporate elements like chlorine or bromine, effectively suppressing flames but raising environmental concerns due to toxicity and potential harm. In contrast, non-halogen types utilize alternative materials, such as phosphorus or nitrogen-based compounds, offering a more environmentally friendly option without compromising performance. The choice between these types often hinges on regulatory compliance, safety requirements, and application-specific performance needs.</p></p>
