<p><strong>The Aluminum Die Casting Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The aluminum die casting machinery market is categorized into three types based on the clamping force exerted during the casting process. Light die casting machines, with a capacity below 4000 kN, are suited for producing smaller, intricate components. Middle die casting machines, ranging from 4000 kN to 10,000 kN, handle medium-sized parts, offering a balance of precision and volume. Heavy die casting machines, exceeding 10,000 kN, are ideal for large, complex components requiring greater force and durability, primarily used in industrial applications.</p></p>
