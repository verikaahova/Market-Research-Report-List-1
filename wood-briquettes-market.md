<p><p>The wood briquettes market is classified into three main types: Solid Cylindrical Type, RUF Type, and Pini-Kay Type. Solid Cylindrical Type briquettes are uniformly shaped and suitable for various applications. RUF Type features a high density, enhancing combustion efficiency and minimizing ash production, making them ideal for heating. Pini-Kay Type briquettes, characterized by their compact rectangular shape and grooves, allow for optimal airflow during burning, promoting a cleaner and more efficient combustion process. Each type caters to specific consumer needs based on performance and usability.</p></p>
