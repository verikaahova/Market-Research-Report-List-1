<p><p>Bellows pumps are specialized devices used to move fluids with precision and control, categorized by temperature handling capabilities. Low temperature bellows pumps are designed for applications requiring the transfer of fluids at sub-zero or low temperatures, such as cryogenics and certain laboratory processes. Each type ensures optimal performance for its respective temperature range.</p></p>
