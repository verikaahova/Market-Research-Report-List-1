<p><p>The viscose yarn market comprises two primary types: Viscose Filament Yarn and Viscose Staple Fiber. Viscose Filament Yarn is a continuous strand used in weaving and knitting, offering a smooth finish and high luster, making it ideal for apparel and luxury textiles. In contrast, Viscose Staple Fiber consists of short strands that are spun into yarn, commonly used in fabrics like denim and home textiles. Both types exhibit properties such as softness, absorbency, and breathability, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
