<p><p>Loop parcel sortation systems encompass various market types, including cross-belt sorters, flat sorters, and tilt tray sorters. Cross-belt sorters utilize horizontal belts to handle parcels efficiently, making them ideal for high-speed sorting. Flat sorters, or bomb bay sorters, rely on a horizontal design to facilitate smooth parcel transitions and sorting accuracy. Tilt tray sorters feature trays that rotate to direct parcels to designated chutes, optimizing space and sorting capacity, which is particularly beneficial in diverse logistics environments.</p></p>
