<p><strong>The Plastic For Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Based</li><li>Powder Based</li><li>Filament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic additive manufacturing market comprises three main types: resin-based, powder-based, and filament materials. Resin-based methods utilize photopolymerized liquids cured by light, ideal for high-detail applications. Powder-based techniques involve layers of powdered material that are fused using heat or binding agents, suitable for producing complex geometries. Filament methods involve thermoplastic strands fed through a nozzle and melted for layer-by-layer construction, popular for its accessibility and versatility in 3D printing, catering to various industries and applications.</p></p>
