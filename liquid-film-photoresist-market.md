<p><strong>The Liquid Film Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist</li><li>Negative Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Film Photoresist market is segmented into two main types: positive and negative photoresists. Positive photoresists become soluble when exposed to light, allowing for precise patterning during photolithography, ideal for detailed circuit designs. In contrast, negative photoresists harden upon exposure, making unexposed areas soluble, which is suitable for applications requiring thicker layers and better resolution. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, printed circuit boards, and microfabrication, catering to various technological and industry needs.</p></p>
