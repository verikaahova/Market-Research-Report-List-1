<p><p>The BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) metalized film market features two primary types: General CPP (Cast Polypropylene) metallized film and Low Temperature Heat Seal CPP metallized film. General CPP metallized film is widely used for packaging due to its excellent barrier properties and visual appeal, making it suitable for food and consumer goods. In contrast, Low Temperature Heat Seal CPP metallized film is specifically designed to seal at lower temperatures, enhancing its usability for sensitive products while maintaining quality and durability.</p></p>
